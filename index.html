Magic Gardener Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 16, 2026

Magic Gardener is developed by Xuanlink. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the game.

Information We Collect

Magic Gardener does not collect, store, sell, or share personal information from users through the game.

Local Game Data

Magic Gardener may store game progress, settings, level progress, inventory, tutorial status, and garden layout locally on your device. This data is used only to provide gameplay features and is not transmitted to us.

Third-Party Services

Magic Gardener is distributed through Google Play. Google Play may process certain information according to Google's own privacy policy for app distribution, security, platform functionality, and crash-related diagnostics.

Magic Gardener does not currently use third-party advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, account login services, cloud save services, or in-app purchase services.

Beta Testing Recruitment

If you voluntarily contact us or submit a beta testing form, we may collect your email address, device model, Android version, and feedback only for beta testing communication, tester management, and troubleshooting.

We do not sell this information. You may contact us to request deletion of your beta testing information.

Children's Privacy

Magic Gardener does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

Data Retention and Deletion

Since Magic Gardener does not collect personal information through the game on our servers, we do not retain personal user data from gameplay. Local game data remains on your device unless you delete the app or clear the app data through your device settings.

Beta testing contact information, if voluntarily provided, will only be kept for beta testing purposes and can be deleted upon request.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
